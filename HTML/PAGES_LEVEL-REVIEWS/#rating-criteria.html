Criteria

Layout Essentially the floorplan and sightlines, but also meta aspects like combat hotspots and balance of power between positions.
Construction How does the level geometry prop up (or hinder) the ideas and gameplay in the level? Bad geometry can ruin a good layout.
Aesthetics Textures, lighting, atmosphere, sound, setting. The look and the feel of the map.
Entities How well are entities and dynamic props used? Can be anything from weapon and player spawns, to physics objects, to bounce pads and teleporters.
Aggregate Mean average of the previous four categories.
Personal How I feel about the map, sans criteria.

Ratings

These are rule-of-thumb definitions. Levels often contain good and bad aspects which must be weighed up when assigning scores. Even the most inexperienced mapper can have great ideas.

Non-existent
* Non-functional
** Functional, but incompetent
*** Competent, but boring
