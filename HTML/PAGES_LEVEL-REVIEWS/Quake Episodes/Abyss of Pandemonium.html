Review:

Starts out not great and gets worse. It quickly devolves into an endless corridor shooter with tough monsters around every corner (literally), with the particular low point being aop1m5: The Dark Palace, which doesn't give you anywhere near enough ammo on hard mode.

Episode 2 is better. aop2m1 has some nice looking areas, but the fights are still crappy, while aop2m2 is the high point, feeling like a runic e4m4. Overall the design is less linear; more Labyrinthine and Quakian. You will probably get lost, because the corridors all look the same, but for the first time it feels like you're playing Quake.

aopdm5 is the highlight of the Deathmatch episode.
