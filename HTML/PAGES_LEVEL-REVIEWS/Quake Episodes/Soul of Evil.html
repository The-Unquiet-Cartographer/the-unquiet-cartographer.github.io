Review:

Episode 1 has a unique autumnal medieval fantasy setting with some large outdoor levels. It is far stronger than episode 2, which is basically all underground mines apart from the penultimate level (unfortunately that level is marred by a kill-counter bug on hard difficulty, requiring you to noclip into the sealed niche in the library in order to proceed).

soedm3 is a really neat deathmatch level that feels very Quake III. It has a fairly basic circuitous layout but is visually distinct, has lots of space, and is packed full of secrets. soedm4 also has some nice sinewy paths, great connectivity and really flows.
