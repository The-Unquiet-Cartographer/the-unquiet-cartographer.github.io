Review:

An impressive episode that is challenging mainly due to its very high enemy count. The levels don't feel designed for combat, which compounds the challenge when there are so many sniping enemies, but the maps are best approached as large doom-style maps, where your first priorities should be to evade enemies, provoke infighting, and collect resources. nsoe2 is by far the weakest map, so don't let it put you off.

While this pack seems unfairly challenging at times, it does offset that challenge by giving the player some fun new toys to play with. The Chain Thunderbolt is lethal. The MIRV rocket chases the crosshair while the fire button is held, then detonates into several smaller homing rockets. The Mjolnir can be thrown by holding the fire button.
