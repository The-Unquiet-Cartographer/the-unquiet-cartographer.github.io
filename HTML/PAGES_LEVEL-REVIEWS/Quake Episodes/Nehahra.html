Review:

A landmark for the Quake modding scene and machinima, but the gameplay was definitely surpassed by other landmark efforts like Zerst√∂rer and Beyond Belief. Nonetheless this map pack has some enjoyable, very atmospheric levels, especially in the second episode. neh1m4 is a quality level, while neh1m6 is memorable more as a gauntlet-style level, where running is preferable to gunning. I'm not a fan of the final boss fight, which is basically just a deathmatch where you have to kill the opponent 6 times to win.

Episode [3/4] - the documentation says episode 4 but the naming scheme says episode 3 - is similar in atmosphere to episode 2 and a worthy addon, although it doesn't really continue the story (if you care about that sort of thing). It does have some issues with fullbright pixels but whatever.

The other big thing with this mod are the new weapons. The Autoshotgun is absolutely overpowered and a blast to use, especially against tougher enemies like fiends, while the Sprocket spears enemies with delayed-fuse rockets. Its uses are not immediately apparent, but it does let you stick enemies with explosives at point blank range and then gives the chance to run away before they detonate.
