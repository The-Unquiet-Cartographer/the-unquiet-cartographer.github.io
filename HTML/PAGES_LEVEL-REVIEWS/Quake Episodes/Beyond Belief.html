Review:

A classic episode well-deserving of its reputation. The outdoor areas in bbelief4 & bbelief5 are particularly memorable.

A couple of minor niggles:
1) I'm convinced the spike trap in bbelief6 is broken. It teleports me out, but then straight back in to get killed. You have to noclip out of it.
2) The final boss has way too much health, and the final level has too little ammo to deal with all the shamblers as well as Chthon.
