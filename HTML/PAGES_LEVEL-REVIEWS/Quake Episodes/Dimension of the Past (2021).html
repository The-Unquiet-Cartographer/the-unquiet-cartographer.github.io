Review:

Bundled with the "Remastered" edition, this version of the episode includes one new map - e5sm1: The Forgotten Castle - and reassigns e5m8: The House of Doom to e5sm2. I can understand why e5sm1 might have been left out of the original release; it's very bare-bones compared to the rest of the episode.

This 2021 version has many of the same issues as Dimension of the Machine - that is, they were made to run on the KEX engine and as of 2022 source port support is still limited. The maps do seem to run without crashing in QuakeSpasm 0.95.1, at least.
