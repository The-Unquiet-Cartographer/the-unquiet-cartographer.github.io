Review:

One of the greatest level packs ever created for Quake. zer1m6 is a map for the ages and there are several other memorable levels. Overall it achieves much of what Nehahra would three years later, and is much more fun to play. Even the shitty graveyard level is more memorable than anything Nehahra has to offer.

The Chainsaw and Riot Controller are excellent additions to the Quake Arsenal. The Chaingun is a hitscan replacement for the Nailgun, which is nice, and the Chain Lightning Gun is pretty awesome as well.
