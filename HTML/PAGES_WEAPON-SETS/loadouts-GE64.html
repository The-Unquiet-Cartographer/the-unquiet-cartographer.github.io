GoldenEye on N64 doesn't let you create custom weapon sets (GoldenEye X does!). However, certain weapon sets work better on certain levels, and the weapon sets in general can be grouped into four categories:

  1. Soviet weapons, feat. DD44, Klobb KF7, other
  2. Mines, feat PP7, ZMG, AR33, other
  3. Power weapons, feat DD44, CMag, other, other
  4. Misc sets that follow their own logic
