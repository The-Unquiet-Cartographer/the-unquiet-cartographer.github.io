Notes - Carrington Villa

The long sight-lines make precision weapons very effective.

The slot [2] weapons are relatively sequestered. You could either use a filler weapon, like the CMP or the Magnum, or a hi-explosive weapon like the SuperDragon or Devastator.

The CMP in [3] is boring but practical.

The tight corners make explosive weapons a good tactical choice without feeling overpowered. The Laptop Gun is a good choice for similar reasons.

The K7 Avenger is excellent for long range and close-quarters alike, and pairs up well with the Laptop Gun.

The Shotgun is garbage.

Weapon counts with 1x Disabled: [1] x3 [2] x3 [3] x3 [4] x3 [5] x3
The 1s and 4s are weighted towards the "front" of the level (pier), while the 2s and 3s are weighted towards the "back". The 5s are evenly spread out. None of the slots have a clearly-defined role, so this set is kind-of bland.

Notes - Chicago

The Sniper Rifle is underpowered here.

Automatics can be overpowered here, so try to mix up the ammo types.

Avoid having the Phoenix alongside the Mauler; the extra ammo makes the Mauler very overpowered.

[1x Disabled] - The shotgun in [2] works really well.

You really want 2x shields to compliment more powerful weapons like the K7 or the SuperDragon.
