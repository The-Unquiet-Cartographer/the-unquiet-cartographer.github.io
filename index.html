Background

I grew up playing TimeSplitters 2, and later discovered the competetive Quake scene. Since then, I've always thought it would be cool to hold a competetive TimeSplitters tournament. I had the idea of creating custom weapon sets, tailored and balanced for each level. This project would later spin off to include other games, and multiplayer level reviews.

I've also designed a few levels, though not many of them have reached completion. The ones that have, I may post here at some point.
