About

I grew up playing TimeSplitters 2, and later discovered the Quake duel scene.

Years ago, I thought it would be really cool to hold a TimeSplitters tournament, so I created custom competetive weapon sets for each level.

Since then, I've played countless first-person shooters. I love exploring new levels, discovering secrets, and learning about the weapon/enemy dynamics in each game.

I've even designed a few levels myself! And actually, I still think it would be cool to hold a TimeSplitters tournament XD
