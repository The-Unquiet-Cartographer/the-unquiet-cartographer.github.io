Background

I grew up playing TimeSplitters 2, and later became an avid spectator of the Quake duel scenes. I've long dreamed of holding a competetive TimeSplitters tournament.

The weapon sets suggested here are each tailored for a specific level, and are designed to be as balanced as possible.

Unfortunately, not all these levels lend themselves to competetive play - being thematically diverse and meant to serve multiple gameplay modes - but the options that work well have been highlighted for your convenience.
