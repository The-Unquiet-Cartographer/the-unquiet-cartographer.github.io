Background

As an avid spectator of the Quake duel scene(s), and a longtime lover of TimeSplitters 2, I've naturally fantasised about a competetive TimeSplitters tournament.

In contrast to a series like Quake though, these games were built with customised multiplayer in mind, with thematically diverse levels that serve multiple gameplay modes.

The weapon sets suggested here are each tailored for a specific level, and designed to be as balanced as possible. Unfortunately, not all levels are created equal, and some do not lend themselves to competetive play; however, options that work particularly well have been highlighted for your convenience.
